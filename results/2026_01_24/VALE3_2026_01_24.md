### 6. Legal, Regulatory, and Risk Factors

**Ongoing Legacy Risks from Dam Disasters**

The most significant legal and regulatory risks for Vale stem from the ongoing consequences of the Brumadinho (2019) and Mariana (2015) dam collapses.

*   **Legal Proceedings:** There are ongoing legal proceedings in Brazil and Europe related to these disasters. These lawsuits could result in significant financial liabilities for the company. The company and some of its current and former executives are also facing criminal charges in Brazil.

*   **Reputational and ESG Impact:** These events continue to have a major reputational impact on Vale. The company is under intense scrutiny regarding its environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices. In response, Vale has emphasized its commitment to dam safety and reparation efforts.

The potential financial and reputational impacts of these legal and regulatory issues remain a material risk for investors.

The overall short-term outlook for VALE3 is **positive**. The dominant pressures are currently favorable, driven by a confluence of factors:
