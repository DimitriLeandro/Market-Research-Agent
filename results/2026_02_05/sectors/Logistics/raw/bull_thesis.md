*   **Trade Expansion:** Domestic and international trade activity continues to be a core driver of Brazil's logistics market. Manufacturers, agricultural producers, retailers, and exporters are increasingly dependent on reliable freight networks to move raw materials and finished goods efficiently across long distances.
*   **Infrastructure Investments:** Significant investments in multimodal infrastructure are enhancing operational efficiencies and responsiveness to consumer needs.
*   **Increased Trade Activities:** The expanding economy fosters increased trade activities both domestically and internationally.
*   **Rising Agricultural Output:** Increasing agricultural output is contributing to the expansion of the freight and logistics market.
