### KNSC11 Investment Thesis Breakdown

**Fund Overview:** KNSC11 is a Brazilian real estate investment fund (FII) managed by Kinea Investimentos. It primarily invests in real estate-backed securities, specifically Certificados de Recebíveis Imobiliários (CRIs) and quotas of other FIIs. The fund is of the ANBIMA renda gestão ativa type, focusing on securities and values.
