The project includes opening new mining areas, implementing new processing lines, duplicating the long-distance belt conveyor (TCLD), and expanding storage areas. Total investments for the project are estimated at $2.8 billion, and it is expected to be commissioned in the second half of 2026.

The Serra Sul 120 project will increase Vale's total production capacity in the Northern System to 260Mtpa.
